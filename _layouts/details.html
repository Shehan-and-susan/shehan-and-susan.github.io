Hotel Accommodations

Courtyard by Marriott Richmond Scott’s Addition

2907 W Broad St, Richmond, VA 23230

Reference: Rajapakse-Fore Wedding Block

Distance from Venue: Approximately 2.5 miles

We've reserved a block of rooms at the Courtyard by Marriott in Scott’s Addition, a vibrant neighborhood known for its eclectic mix of restaurants, breweries, and entertainment options. This hotel offers a perfect base for exploring Richmond, with plenty of local spots just a short walk or drive away. Scott’s Addition is famous for its craft breweries, including Ardent Craft Ales, The Veil Brewing Co., and Hardywood Park Craft Brewery, all within a mile of the hotel. If you're in the mood for a bite, you'll find fantastic restaurants like ZZQ Texas Craft Barbeque, Cochiloco, and Lunch & Supper available nearby.

For those looking to enjoy Richmond's vibrant nightlife, you'll be pleased to know that this area also boasts several trendy bars and cocktail lounges, perfect for unwinding after a day of wedding festivities. Plus, with the hotel only 2.5 miles away from the venue at Maymont, getting to and from the ceremony and reception will be a breeze.

Be sure to mention the Fore-Rajapakse Wedding Block when booking to receive the special rate. We can't wait to celebrate with you in Richmond!

Things to do in Richmond

Places to Visit

  • Maymont – A beautiful estate with gardens, wildlife exhibits, and a historic mansion.
  • Virginia Museum of Fine Arts – A world-class art museum with diverse collections.
  • Hollywood Cemetery – Historic cemetery with graves of notable figures and scenic views of the James River.
  • Lewis Ginter Botanical Garden – Stunning gardens and a conservatory.

Restaurants

  • Lemaire – Fine dining at The Jefferson Hotel.
  • Stella's – Delicious Greek cuisine.
  • Can Can Brasserie – French-inspired brasserie in Carytown.
  • Mama J's Kitchen – Soul food in Jackson Ward.

Experiences

  • Riverfront Canal Walk – A scenic walk along the canal in downtown Richmond.
  • James River Park – Outdoor activities like kayaking, hiking, and picnicking.
  • The Poe Museum – Explore the life and works of Edgar Allan Poe.
  • Shockoe Slip – Historic district with shops, restaurants, and nightlife.
